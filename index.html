Jason Ku Wang

I'm a first-year medical student at Harvard & MIT's HST program. Previously, I studied Mathematical & Computational Science at Stanford and Public Policy at Tsinghua as a Schwarzman Scholar. At Stanford, I co-founded the university's annual healthcare hackathon (Health++) and organized CS 522: Seminar in Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare. In industry, I've worked on large-scale machine learning at Facebook and Tableau, product management at HealthTap, and public affairs at Johnson & Johnson. Get in touch if you're interested in applications of artificial intelligence to medicine!
