Jason Ku Wang

I'm an incoming medical student at Harvard. Previously, I studied Mathematical & Computational Science at Stanford and Public Policy at Tsinghua as a Schwarzman Scholar. At Stanford, I co-founded the university's annual healthcare hackathon (Health++) and organized CS 522: Seminar in Artificial Intelligence in Healthcare. In industry, I've worked on machine learning at Facebook and Tableau, and health policy at Johnson & Johnson. Get in touch if you're interested in applications of artificial intelligence to medicine!
